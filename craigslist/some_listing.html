We are looking to rent our house on a month-to-month basis starting on/about Aug. 31.
It is a large (1600 sq ft), charming, eclectic, rustic house located in the Casa Solana neighborhood. It features 3BD 2BA, a living room with a kiva fireplace, an eat-in kitchen, a sunny foyer, and a large open-area office space. The backyard is perfect for eating outside under the plum or apple trees, napping in the hammock, and sitting by a fire in the chimenea.
One of the really great things about the house is its location. It is within 400 yards of 3 city parks. The Bicentennial Park has tennis courts, basketball courts, soccer/baseball/football fields, and a children's playground. Within a stone's throw is the Santa Fe River trail, which is a smoothly paved 2.5-mile sidewalk that extends from Frenchy's Park to St. Francis St. and is perfect for strolling, bicycling, or roller-blading. In fact, one can easily bike or walk into town. La Montanita Co-op natural food store is 10 minutes down the street by foot. The Devargas Mall and the Railyard Plaza are a 20-minute walk away and feature movie theatres, excellent dining options, and interesting art galleries. Mountain bikers will be relieved to know that the La Tierra trail system and its various technical courses are a short ride away. For people who want to leave their cars in the driveway, the walk/bike score of this neighborhood must be one of the best in the city!

PLEASE NOTE that the house is currently for sale. We are offering a reduced rent of $1200/mo for the right tenant in order to accommodate realtors coming by to show the house (to date, it's been about once/week). The realtor would call you the day before to coordinate, and the requirement would be for you to have to the house show-ready ie: neat & tidy, no dirty dishes in sink, beds made, bathrooms clean, and so on for the appointed time. Most showings last about 1 hour during which time you will need to be out of the house.

Please reply via email and tell us a little about yourself and what your situation is.
